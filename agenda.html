PRAC Delegates and Accompanying Guests are cordially invited to join Carey partners and guests for a special evening in our very special Atacama “home”.

Following our visit to the Valley of the Moon, we will return to the Hotel for a reception, and for those who wish to join, the evening will also include a stargazing event at the hotel.

“Changes and Challenges of Community Relations in Mining and Power Sectors”

Many jurisdictions, including Chile, have seen a rising tide of social opposition to development projects, especially in the mining and power sectors, in recent years. Today’s communities are insisting on a different kind of treatment.

Join PRAC Delegates from various jurisdictions including Australia, Canada, Chile and Peru to learn some of the latest developments in managing community relations.
